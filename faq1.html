Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MANC Riders delivery service

MANC Riders provides fast and reliable delivery services. You can create a shipment from your dashboard, add customer details, pickup address, payment information and submit your parcel for delivery. Same-day and fragile delivery options are available.

Delivery charges depend on location, parcel size, weight and service type. COD fees may vary according to your package category.

We provide nationwide coverage with expanding support for urban and regional areas.

Use the tracking ID in your dashboard or tracking page to monitor parcel status in real time.
